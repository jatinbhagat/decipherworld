🧠 Innovation Mindset

✅ Do This:
  • • Ask "What if...?" questions
  • • Build on others' ideas
  • • Try wild and crazy solutions
  • • Learn from "failures"
  • • Focus on helping people
❌ Avoid This:
  • • Saying "That won't work"
  • • Judging ideas too quickly
  • • Worrying about being perfect
  • • Staying in your comfort zone
  • • Working alone
