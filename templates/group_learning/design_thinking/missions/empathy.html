🌟 Key Learnings

• Observation reveals hidden problems
• Emotions are as important as actions
• Assumptions can be wrong
• Real empathy takes time and attention

🚀 Apply This Learning

Empathy skills are valuable beyond design thinking. You can use observation and empathy when:

Understanding Friends:

Notice when friends are struggling, even when they don't say anything

Family Dynamics:

Understand what family members really need beyond their words

School Projects:

Design anything for others by understanding their real needs

🎯 Empathy Success Tips

✓ Focus on one specific user type
✓ Notice both physical and emotional responses
✓ Look for workarounds and adaptations
✓ Ask "why" to understand deeper motivations

⚠️ Avoid These Mistakes

✗ Making assumptions without observing
✗ Focusing only on what people say
✗ Ignoring emotions and only noting actions
✗ Rushing the observation process
