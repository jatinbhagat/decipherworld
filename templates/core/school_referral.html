Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our school referral program

Who can participate in the referral program?

Anyone can participate! Whether you're a parent, teacher, educator, or someone who knows schools that could benefit from AI-powered education, you're welcome to refer schools.

What schools are eligible for the program?

All schools are welcome regardless of grade levels or student count. The school must not currently be using DecipherWorld and must be genuinely interested in implementing AI-powered education solutions.

When do I receive the ₹50,000 reward?

You'll receive the reward after the referred school makes their first payment and officially starts using DecipherWorld's platform. Payment is processed within 30 days of conversion.

How long does the referral process take?

We contact the school within 2-3 business days. The complete process from contact to conversion typically takes 2-8 weeks, depending on the school's decision timeline.

Can I refer multiple schools?

Absolutely! You can refer as many eligible schools as you want. Each successful referral earns you ₹50,000, so your earning potential is unlimited.
